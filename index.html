Our Mission
Who We Are

Our Mission

Innovation Thru Energy (ITE) is dedicated to revolutionizing cold-chain logistics by combining green technology and digitalization. With a focus on sustainability and SDG-aligned efficiency, ITE transforms the way temperature-sensitive goods are transported and stored across the globe.

The IceBattery® system replaces refrigerated trucks, dry ice, and power-hungry cold storage with a versatile, passive phase-change solution — serving food, pharmaceuticals, vaccines, medical supplies, and specialty cargo across all modes: air, rail, land, and sea.

Where We're Headed

Our Vision

To become the global standard for eco-friendly cold chain solutions — a world where temperature-sensitive logistics operates with zero carbon emissions and zero reliance on electrical power or dry ice.

ITE is actively building Indo-Japan cold chain corridors, partnering with CONCOR on Dedicated Freight Corridors, and embedding AI-powered IB-Trace® monitoring to create the most transparent, sustainable supply chain ecosystem on earth.
